1. Information We Collect

At Key2YourHome, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect essential information required to provide our services, and we do not collect any personal data unless you voluntarily create an account on our platform.

Account Information

  • Your name, email address, and contact number provided during account registration
  • Information related to saved searches, preferences, or favorite listings

Technical Information

  • Basic session data such as browser type and device used
  • We do not collect IP addresses, location data, or device fingerprints
  • No third-party tracking or marketing cookies are used

Cookie Policy

  • We do not use cookies for advertising, marketing, or personal tracking
  • Cookies are used only to maintain your logged-in session (if applicable)
  • You may use the platform without enabling cookies for non-authenticated browsing

We respect your privacy and are fully transparent about the limited data we collect. No information is shared with third-party advertisers, and your activity on the platform is not tracked beyond necessary operational functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

To provide personalized property recommendations and connect you with real estate professionals.

Improveing User Experience

To facilitate property & projects searching and bookmarking and other user managements.

Security & Fraud Prevention

To verify identities, prevent fraud, and protect against unauthorized access.

Service Improvement

To analyze usage patterns and enhance our platform's features and performance.

Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties .

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

End-to-End Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Strict Access Controls

Limited employee access to personal data based on role requirements.

Regular Security Audits

Ongoing vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Typical retention periods are:

  • Active accounts: Until account deletion request
  • Transaction records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Inactive accounts: 3 years of inactivity

4. Cookies Policy

Key2YourHome values your privacy. We only use cookies that are strictly necessary to ensure the core functionality of our website. We do not use any cookies for marketing, analytics, or tracking purposes.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for security, authentication, and basic system operations Session

Cookie Management

Since we only use essential cookies, there are no cookies to manage or opt out of. These are required to keep our site functional and secure. No advertising, marketing, or analytics cookies are set by our system.

5. Your Rights

As a user of Key2YourHome, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your personal data.

Right to Data Portability

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@key2yourhome.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request, which we will do within 30 days.
