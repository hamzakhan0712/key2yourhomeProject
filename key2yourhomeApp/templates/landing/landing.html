Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a property visit?

You can schedule a visit by clicking the “Schedule Visit” button on any listing page. Pick your preferred time and our agent will confirm with you shortly.

Are the property listings updated in real-time?

Yes, our listings are updated in real-time based on availability and market changes, ensuring you always have the latest information.

What documents do I need to buy a property?

Typically, you will need your government ID, proof of income, address proof, and loan approval (if applicable). Our agents will assist you with the exact list based on the property.

Can I get help with a home loan?

Yes, we partner with leading banks and NBFCs to offer home loan assistance. You can apply directly from our website or consult our finance advisor.

Are there any hidden charges?

We believe in transparency. All charges, including registration, taxes, and brokerage (if any), will be clearly listed before you make a decision.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach out to us via the chat widget, call us directly, or use the Contact Us page. We respond within 24 hours.

Do you assist with rental properties?

Yes, our platform also includes verified rental listings and our agents can help with agreements, background checks, and smooth onboarding for tenants.
