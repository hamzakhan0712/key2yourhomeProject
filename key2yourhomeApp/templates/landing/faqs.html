General Questions

What is Key2YourHome?

Key2YourHome is a comprehensive real estate platform developed by InitCore Developers Group that connects buyers, sellers, renters, and landlords in a seamless digital environment. Our platform offers advanced search tools, virtual tours, and secure transaction processing to simplify the property buying, selling, and renting process.

How does Key2YourHome differ from other real estate platforms?

Key2YourHome stands out through our:

  • Advanced AI-powered property matching system
  • Integrated virtual tour technology with 3D walkthroughs
  • Secure document handling and digital signing
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • 24/7 customer support with real estate experts
  • Neighborhood analytics and market trends

Is Key2YourHome available in my area?

Key2YourHome is continuously expanding to new markets. Currently, we operate in all 50 U.S. states and major international markets including Canada, the UK, and Australia. To check specific coverage in your area:

  1. Visit our coverage map on the homepage
  2. Enter your city or zip code in the search bar
  3. Contact our support team for specific inquiries

We're adding new locations regularly, so even if we're not in your area yet, we likely will be soon!

Account Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account with Key2YourHome is simple:

Option 1: Email Registration

  1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  2. Select "Register with Email"
  3. Fill in your details
  4. Verify your email address

Option 2: Social Login

  1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  2. Select your preferred social platform
  3. Authorize the connection
  4. Complete your profile

Accounts are free to create and give you access to saved searches, property alerts, and more.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password" below the login form
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link (valid for 24 hours)
  5. Click the link and create a new secure password

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
